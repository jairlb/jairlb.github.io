Applying Knowledge and the Life Sciences

Througout my two years pursuing my undergraduate at UCSD and presently, I have gained experience working in the life sciences through working at at a local startup that offers technological solutions and assistance for clinical trials in 1-3 phases.
I am fortuante to have this experience as it has demonstrated to me the syntheseis between abstract technology, software engineering, and product applications while allowing me to build skills as a working in a formal team. I have been able to witness and be a part of software creation from planning to launch, which has reinforced to me the influential effects that abstract sciences can have in the medical field and life sciences.
My experience, along with my interdisciplinary and mixed cognitive science and computer science background, has me excited and eager to continue growing my skills and being apart of this sort of progress in the future.
